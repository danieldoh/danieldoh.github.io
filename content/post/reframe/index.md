## Problem 
- Security & Privacy (S&P) threats due to how AR devices capture and process the users’ environments

## Solution
**AR storyboarding tool:**
  - Adopts a character-driven analysis approach
  - Frame-based authoring technique
    - Focus on prototyping AR interactions 
    - Refines storyboard elements that are subject to potential threats

## Requirements
- Simulating different AR usage environments
  - Easily simulating 
- Supporting rapid prototyping of Ar interaction techniques and modalities
  - Rapid prototyping
  - Designers to experiment with AR-specific and multimodal interactions
- Highlighting proxemic interactions between AR users, non-users, and the physical & virtual spaces
  - Framed based authoring
- Providing abstractions for understanding threat models and support for brainstorming solutions 
  - No formal training required for S&P
  - Character-driven analysis

## System
- Frame-based authoring approach
  - Captures and enhances storyboard elements which are relevant for threat modeling
- Character-driven analysis tools
  - Provide abstractions for understanding S&P issues by personifying classes of threats in our underlying threat model
